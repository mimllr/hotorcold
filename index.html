Flashback

Remember when you used to play that game with your annoying sibling? "Getting warmer!" Now you can play using your phone's GPS.

How To Play

Someone initiates the game by clicking the "Start Game" button. Once your location is established, you'll be given a link to text your friends.

When they load the page, it'll calculate how far away they are, and tell them how hot or cold they're getting!

The game ends when everyone gets hot!
